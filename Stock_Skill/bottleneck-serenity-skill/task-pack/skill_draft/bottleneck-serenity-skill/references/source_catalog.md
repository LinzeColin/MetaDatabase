## Interpretation notes

- Public social-media performance statements are not audited evidence.
- Community repositories are implementations and experiments, not peer-reviewed validation.
- Academic anomalies can decay after publication and may not survive transaction costs or different markets.
- The source catalog supports methodology design; every live investment case still requires current primary evidence.
