# TAB FIFA Research App Icon

Concept: Research Compass.

The icon combines three signals:

- football: the central white ball;
- market research: radar rings and pitch-grid geometry;
- value discipline: small gold value marks, restrained and precise.

The icon intentionally avoids casino chips, dollar signs, tickets, or betting-slip imagery.
It should feel like a professional research terminal for football probability and market analysis.

Palette:

- deep teal background;
- emerald/cyan analytical lines;
- off-white football core;
- gold value accents;
- soft shadow for macOS Dock readability.
