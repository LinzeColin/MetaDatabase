3. 机制拆解

In this work, we introduce PolyWorkBench, a benchmark for evaluating LLM agents on multilingual long-horizon workplace workflows. To enable comprehensive evaluation, we propose a hybrid framework that combines structural grading, executable verification, and LLM-based semantic assessment. This design allows us to capture both functional correctness and linguistic consistency across complex workflows.

4. 证据与数字

PolyWorkBench consists of 67 tasks across five domains, including commerce, knowledge work, legal analysis, localization, and manufacturing, where agents must process heterogeneous multilingual inputs, perform iterative reasoning, invoke external tools, and produce structured outputs.
