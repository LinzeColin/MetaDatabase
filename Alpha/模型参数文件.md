### FORM-001

- model_id: `MOD-001`
- expression_or_pseudocode: `For each symbol and lookback in (5,10,20): total_return=end/start-1; drawdown=min(close/running_max-1); oos_return and hit_rate come from positive momentum one-step-forward windows; score=total_return+0.5*oos_return+0.01*hit_rate-abs(drawdown); rank desc by (decision=='promote_to_paper', score).`
- evidence_refs: `EVID-ALPHA-FORMULA-REGISTRY`

### FORM-002
