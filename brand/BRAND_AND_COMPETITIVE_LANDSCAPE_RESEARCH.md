## Competitive Pattern Summary

The v5 package covers 49 representative mature products across supply-chain risk, electronic supply-chain data, third-party risk, logistics visibility, planning/digital twin, graph investigation, corporate/market intelligence and trade/entity data.

EEI should not try to replace Bloomberg, S&P Capital IQ, Orbis, Palantir, mature supply-chain-risk systems or specialist data vendors in v0.1. Its MVP differentiation is:

> A recursive company-centered research workspace that connects corporate ecosystem, supply chain, control, capital, policy, strategy, evidence versions, model parameters and time snapshots in one explainable environment.

## Formal EEI Brand Clearance Gate

Before public commercial use, complete and store evidence for:

- CN/US/EU/UK/AU trademark knockout search.
- Company-name and product-name search.
- Domain, social handle and SEO conflict search.
- App Store and Google Play search.
- GitHub, npm, PyPI and package-namespace search.
- Phonetic, semantic and negative-meaning review in Chinese and English.
- Same-category product and adjacent-category product review.
- Legal counsel sign-off or an explicit signed risk waiver.

Until that gate is closed, EEI remains the repository and system identity, but public brand claims must be treated as un-cleared.
